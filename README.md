
# [Tech Haven - Your Gateway to Tech Bliss](https://techhaven-1014.web.app/)



Welcome to Tech Haven, where technology meets excellence. At Tech Haven, we are your trusted source for the latest mobile phones and laptops. Our mission is to provide you with cutting-edge devices, outstanding customer service, and unbeatable value. Explore a haven of innovation and discover the perfect tech companions for your lifestyle. Join the Tech Haven community and elevate your tech experience today.

### - [Live Site](https://techhaven-1014.web.app/)






## Tech Stack

**Client:** React, React Router, TailwindCSS, DaisyUi, Firebase

**Server:** Node, Express


## Features

- Password Validation
- One Click Login with (Google & Facebook)
- Theme Swap
- Delete Product From Dashboard
- [Dashboard](https://techhaven-1014.web.app/dashboard/profile)
- [Add Product](https://techhaven-1014.web.app/add-product)
- [View Products List](https://techhaven-1014.web.app/dashboard/view-product)
